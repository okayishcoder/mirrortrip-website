What Mirror Trip Does

More than a trip planner. It is where your journey keeps evolving.

Start simple with a rough outline or go deep with a full itinerary. Mirror Trip keeps your plans, live updates, and completed journeys together so every trip stays useful before, during, and after travel.

Plan from rough to detailed

Start with destinations, dates, travellers, and notes, then build day by day with travel segments, activities, locations, costs, and personal details.

Turn plans into living records

Keep updating your trip as it happens so the original plan becomes a real record of where you went, what you did, and what mattered most.

Keep your full travel life together

Organize future plans, active trips, and completed journeys in one place that is easy to revisit, relive, and reuse for the next adventure.

Share guides worth saving

Publish completed trips with routes, highlights, notes, and practical insights so other travelers can get inspired and add them to their wishlist.

Why Mirror Trip

Built for planning smarter, traveling better, and remembering more.

Mirror Trip is designed for travelers who want flexibility. Keep a trip high level when you are still figuring things out, then add structure and detail when you are ready to lock it in.

When the journey is over, your trip is not just done. It becomes a searchable, photo-filled memory you can revisit yourself or publish to help someone plan theirs.

Flexible by design Plan loosely at first or build a complete itinerary when details matter.
Useful during the trip Update routes, notes, costs, and photos as travel unfolds in real time.
Valuable after the trip Revisit memories or publish your trip as a guide others can discover.
