Support

Help for Mirror Trip users and app reviewers.

For support requests, privacy questions, deletion or restoration questions, and bug reports, contact the Mirror Trip support team.

support@mirrortrips.com

What support can help with

  • account access or account-related questions
  • account deletion and restoration requests during the grace period
  • privacy-related requests
  • bug reports or unexpected app behaviour
  • general questions about using Mirror Trip

Best way to contact support

Email support@mirrortrips.com and include enough detail for the team to understand the request.

For account-specific issues, sending the request from your account email address may help the team verify and respond more efficiently.

FAQ

Common support questions

How do I request account deletion?

Account deletion is requested from within the app. The public deletion page explains what happens immediately, during the grace period, and after permanent deletion.

Can a deleted account be restored?

During the 15-day grace period, restoration requests can be sent to support@mirrortrips.com. Restoration is not guaranteed.

Where can I submit a privacy request?

Privacy-related requests can be sent to the same support email. The published privacy policy is available on this site.

Are public store download links available here?

This site does not claim live store listings unless official store links are published separately.
